About Gerson Solulu Scholarship

The Gerson Solulu Scholarship Scheme supports Papua New Guinean students who demonstrate academic potential and financial need. We partner with institutions to make higher learning accessible and to build leaders for local communities.

Our Mission

To provide financial support and opportunities for talented students from underserved communities across Morobe Province so they can complete tertiary education and contribute to national development.
