About the Scheme

The Gerson Solulu Scholarship Scheme was established to support students across Papua New Guinea who demonstrate academic potential and financial need. We believe education is the key to unlocking opportunity, and our mission is to make higher learning accessible to all.

Whether you're pursuing a degree in teaching, business, engineering, or the arts, our program is designed to help you succeed. We work closely with institutions and officers to ensure transparency, fairness, and impact.
